Our Best Flavours

A Belgian waffle is a delicious breakfast or dessert treat known for its light and fluffy texture. It is characterized by its deep grid pattern and is typically enjoyed with toppings such as fresh fruit, syrup, whipped cream, or chocolate.

A berry waffle is a delightful variation of the classic waffle, infused with the vibrant flavors of fresh berries. It features juicy berries mixed into the batter or served as a topping, adding a burst of sweetness and tanginess. The combination of the crispy waffle and the fruity goodness makes for a scrumptious and refreshing treat.

A blueberry cheesecake waffle is a heavenly fusion of two beloved desserts. It combines the indulgent creaminess of cheesecake with the sweetness of blueberries, all piled onto a warm, crispy waffle. The result is a delightful symphony of flavors, creating a decadent and unforgettable culinary experience.

A brownie waffle is a divine indulgence that combines the best of both worlds: the fudgy richness of a brownie and the crispy texture of a waffle. It offers a delightful blend of chocolatey goodness and a satisfying crunch, making it a heavenly treat for chocolate lovers and waffle enthusiasts alike.

A Kitkat waffle is a decadent delight that marries the irresistible flavors of a kitkat with the crispy perfection of a waffle. It boasts a rich, moist interior with a slightly crisp exterior, creating a heavenly combination of textures. Topped with a dollop of whipped cream or a drizzle of chocolate sauce, it's a dessert lover's dream come true.

A red velvet waffle is a luxurious twist on the classic waffle, infused with the distinct flavors of red velvet cake. With its vibrant red color and subtle hints of cocoa, it offers a velvety texture and a slightly tangy taste. Topped with cream cheese frosting or powdered sugar, it's a decadent treat for any occasion.
